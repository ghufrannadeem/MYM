Mission

Our mission is to help ten million black women go vegan in the next ten years.

There are 22 million black women and girls in the U.S. and our goal is to change our health paradigm now and for generations to come.

The 10 Million Black Vegan Women Movement builds on the extraordinary success of the 10,000 Black Vegan Women program launched in 2020 by vegan changemaker Tracye McQuirter, MPH, when more than 15,000 women signed up for the first Free 21-Day Vegan Fresh Start and experienced unprecedented health benefits.
