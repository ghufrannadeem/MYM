Make Your Mark Training and Consulting Inc. have been assisting couples who own businesses together since 2004. The company was founded by Colin & Gabi Sprake who have been in business together for over 20 years and they know what it truly takes to work together day-in and day-out.

We have been assisting businesses that are in the start up phase through to businesses that are making $3,000,000 in annual revenues. We are experts in profitability, strategy, growth, systems, structure, procedures, efficiencies, scaling and exiting or succession planning.

Colin is the business genius in their replation and has owned, operated and sold numerous businesses over the past 30-years. Gabi brings her gifts of creativity, support and implementation that has made majority of their ventures successful. Plus, she has a kindness and gentleness about her that is magnetic.

Sadly things have not always worked out as planned and some ventures hit a wall which really tested their fortitude and relationship. On the positive side, they are extremely grateful for every challenge they have experienced because it has allowed them to grow and learn, and now help businesses in the same position.
